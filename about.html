Elementary Schools

Spiritridge Elementary is the main elementary school served by Sammamish View. Walking Distance is 5-6 minutes, car takes 2-3 minutes, served by Bus Route 37

Moreover, though there are more elemantries, this school is the closest and supports both AL and Gen Ed students. The Bus Route 37 is a route that serves other neighbourhoods like 161 (Wildridge Park) and 48th Street and 152

There are 2 middle schools served by Sammamish View. BSD's Advanced Learning program, essentially divides the middle schools. Mainly, Gen Ed students go to Tillicum Middle School while AL mainly go to Odle.
